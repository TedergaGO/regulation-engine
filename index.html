About Me

Gürkan Atabay is an accomplished computer engineer and entrepreneur with over 20 years of experience leading large-scale cloud infrastructure, DevOps, and system integration projects across Africa, Europe, and Central Asia. He holds a B.Sc. in Computer Engineering, a B.A. in Economics, and is a Microsoft Certified Trainer (MCT) with MCSE-level certifications.

In 2020, amidst the global pandemic, he founded Tederga Bilişim and scaled it from a solo startup to a thriving technology company. Through national and international projects, he expanded Tederga's portfolio while mentoring 15+ young talents and launching commercially successful SaaS platforms.

As a board member of Turkey's Open Data and Technology Association (AVTED), he drives civic technology initiatives and has represented the association at the Turkish Grand National Assembly AI Commission. In 2025, Tederga opened a branch at GOSB Teknopark, one of Turkey's most prestigious R&D hubs.
